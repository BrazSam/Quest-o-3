Struggling doorknob salesman Steve breaks into a mine to fulfill a childhood dream, when he discovers the Orb of Dominance and the Earth Crystal. When combined, they create a portal that transports him to the Overworld, a world where the terrain is made of easily manipulated cubes. He builds his own paradise and later stumbles across a portal to a hellish world called the Nether. He is imprisoned by Malgosha, the gold-obsessed piglin ruler of the Nether who hates creativity. Because the Orb would allow her to control the Overworld, Steve has his dog Dennis hide it and the Crystal under his bed in the real world.
